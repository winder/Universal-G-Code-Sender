World of Sci-Fi Font (version 1)

2022 Iconian Fonts - Daniel Zadorozny

http://www.iconian.com

This is a dingbat font with over 80 science fiction related glyphs. Logos, spacecraft, characters and more.

Use of a character map program, such as Windows' Character Map, will be very helpful in exploring and using this font.
